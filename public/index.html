Our Story
A Labour of Love

Verdana was born from a simple belief — that exceptional food should be paired with an equally exceptional atmosphere. Our head chef brings two decades of Michelin-starred experience to every plate, blending classical French technique with the bold flavors of South India.

Every dish is a conversation between tradition and the season. We change our menu quarterly, letting the land guide our kitchen.
